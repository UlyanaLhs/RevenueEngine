12. Payment Methods and Confidentiality

a) Why we prioritize cryptocurrency

Our preferred payment method is cryptocurrency (USDT, TRC-20 network). We operate as an independent, unincorporated practice serving clients internationally, and many mainstream payment processors and banking rails place restrictions on cross-border payments to individual service providers, are unavailable in certain regions, or involve delays, high conversion fees, or account holds that can disrupt a project timeline. Cryptocurrency payments settle quickly, work reliably across borders, and let us keep our pricing predictable for clients regardless of their location.

We also accept Payoneer and other arrangements agreed upon directly with the client, where mutually convenient.

b) Invoicing via Lava.top

For clients who prefer a traditional invoice rather than a direct cryptocurrency transfer, we issue invoices through Lava.top, a payment processing service that supports international clients and multiple payment methods, including card payments. We use Lava.top because it allows us to provide a standard invoice and receipt for accounting purposes while still accommodating clients whose preferred payment method differs from cryptocurrency, without requiring us to hold a merchant account with a traditional bank-dependent processor.

When you pay via Lava.top, your payment information (card details, billing information) is processed directly by Lava.top and its payment partners under their own privacy and security practices; we do not receive or store your full payment card details.

c) Confidentiality of payment information

Cryptocurrency payments are processed through the client's and our own wallets directly; we do not store your wallet address or transaction history on this Site beyond what is necessary for our own accounting records. Any payment details exchanged for invoicing purposes (via Lava.top or otherwise) are kept confidential and used solely for the purposes of completing the transaction and maintaining accurate financial records.
